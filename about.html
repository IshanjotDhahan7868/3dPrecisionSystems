The Story

Precision 3D Technologies started with a conversation I kept having over and over. A business owner would tell me they had a great product, solid leads, and a sales team that worked hard — but their close rate was stuck at 15–20%. They couldn't figure out why.

The answer was almost always the same: the gap between a lead's first interest and their first real conversation with your product is too wide, too slow, and too dependent on your reps' time.

Most B2B product companies ask prospects to take a leap of faith. Here's a brochure. Here's a contact form. Wait 3 days for a quote. Hope they don't go to a competitor in the meantime. That process was designed in 2005 and hasn't changed.

The companies winning in product sales today have closed that gap. They've put an interactive, visual, self-service experience on their website — one that lets buyers design their own product, see the price in real time, and submit a pre-qualified configuration when they're ready. The sales team only gets involved to close.

That's what I build. Not just a 3D viewer, not just a price calculator — but a complete sales experience that moves a browser to a buyer without requiring a rep for the first 80% of the process.

Every project I take on is one I believe has a clear, measurable ROI. If the math doesn't work, I'll tell you before we start. If it does work — and it usually does — you'll see it in your close rate within 60 days.
