Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

Precision 3D Technologies is operated by Ishanjot Dhahan, based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. We build custom interactive 3D product configurators for B2B businesses. You can reach us at ishanjot@outlook.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, we may collect:

  • Your name and business name
  • Your email address and phone number
  • Your company website and product description
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide in the contact form

We also collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type, referring pages) through our hosting provider (Vercel) for security and performance purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule discovery calls
  • Prepare your free 3D configurator mockup
  • Send project-related communications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Calendly — for scheduling discovery calls. Calendly's own privacy policy applies when you book a meeting.
  • Vercel — for hosting. Vercel may collect standard server log data.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Google may log requests for font files.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. If you'd like your data deleted, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ishanjot@outlook.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at ishanjot@outlook.com.
